# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## ✅ Do’s
- Use respectful and professional language
- Think before posting anything
- Keep your profile clean and appropriate
- Verify information before sharing
- Maintain privacy settings

## ❌ Don’ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
- Do not engage in online arguments or hate speech
- Avoid spreading fake news
- Do not misuse others’ content without permission
