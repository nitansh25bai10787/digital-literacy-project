# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never approve a UPI request if you are receiving money
- Do not scan unknown QR codes
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages
- Always verify the identity of the person before making any payment
- Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication
- Do not share OTP, PIN, or banking details with anyone
- Keep your apps and phone updated
- Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi

## UPI Safety Tips
- Remember: You do NOT need to enter PIN to receive money
- Double-check payment requests before approving
